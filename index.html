Uchiha Itachi

The prodigy of the Uchiha clan

Here's a few things that made him a true prodigy among the Uchihas

  • He awakened and mastered the sharingan at the age of 7.
  • He was allowed to take part in the chunin exams which he passed at the age of 10.
  • He entered ANBU black ops at the age of 11
  • He was made a squad captain at age 13
  • Became a double agent and reported his clan's activities to the Konoha elders and the Hokage.
  • Slaughtered his entire clan including his parents for the benefit of the village
  • He unlocked the mangekyo sharingan.
  • Successfully infiltrated Akatsuki and kept it from moving against Konoha.
